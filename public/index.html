Tuition-free curriculum for everyone.

The Internet is filled with free online courses, books, and material which could be used to get the equivalent of a degree from a top university. Equiv.org exists to provide organization to those resources.

Reuse online resources

College and universities are releasing 'open-source' courses every day. We guide you through which courses to take to get the equivalent of any degree.

Work at your pace

Everyone works at their own pace. What takes you 1 month to learn might take others 2 years. equiv.org ensures that time isn't an issue.

Consistent tools

Learning course material often requires help from others taking the same program. We provide the same tools across all your courses for helping you actually learn the material.
